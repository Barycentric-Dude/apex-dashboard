APEX SmartConnect Panel

IoT Add-on Panel for Remote Monitoring & Intelligent Alerts

The APEX SmartConnect Panel is an advanced Industrial IoT Panel built to improve visibility, reliability, and performance of electrical and pumping systems. It integrates with existing Smart Control Panel setups to deliver real-time monitoring, instant alerts, and remote access—helping teams shift from reactive maintenance to proactive system management.

Monitoring Capabilities

What the System Monitors

Designed for critical electrical and pumping operations, the APEX SmartConnect Panel functions as a Remote Monitoring Panel that tracks key signals and equipment health in real time.

  • Pump running status
  • Motor faults and trips
  • Power supply conditions
  • Voltage and current levels
  • Alarm signals
  • Equipment status
