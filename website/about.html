Who We Are

Who We Are

APEX ENERGY CONTROL is a Pune-based industrial technology company dedicated to developing IoT-enabled monitoring solutions for control panels and critical electrical systems.

We specialize in transforming conventional control panels into intelligent, connected systems that provide real-time visibility, instant alerts, and remote access to operational data.

Our solutions are built on a strong foundation of electrical engineering, industrial automation, and smart connectivity.

By integrating IoT technology with practical engineering, we help industries gain better control, faster response, and improved system reliability.

Our focus is on delivering simple, reliable, and application-driven solutions that solve real-world operational challenges.

  • IoT-enabled monitoring for control panels and critical electrical systems
  • Real-time visibility with instant alerts
  • Remote access to operational data for faster response
